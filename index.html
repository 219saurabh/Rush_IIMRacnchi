ABOUT Agon-Rush 2022

The Annual Management, Cultural & Sports Fest of IIM Ranchi..!

Agon-Rush is a 3 day event, which provides a national level platform for the B-school enthusiasts to show their skills be it finance guy, Debater, Writer, sports enthusiast, art, have a start-up in mind, or simply an idea that can make lives better. A power-pack B-school fest with management challenges, cultural and sports events. This is an intercollege fest open for all the B-schools.


about rush

Cultural & Sports Fest of IIM Ranchi

Rush is a slew of art, culture, entertaining games, events, competitions, and activities to get your creative and cerebral juices flowing. All of this is done in a competitive yet pleasant setting in order to foster a healthy competitive spirit, as well as a desire to learn and grow.
